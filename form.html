5 Simple Steps

Please select your basic choices from the drop-downs below to get started. Each drop-down menu has a thumbnail to give you an idea of what the choice looks like.

Please feel free to contact us with any questions! You may use the chat box in the bottom right corner of your screen that says 'Online' or you may send us a message.



1. Choose Your Leather Color


2. Choose Your Type of Hardware

All of our jackets are made with highly durable, industry tested YKK brand hardware from Japan.


3. Choose Your Lining

Choose from a variety of colored, patterned and quilted linings to best compliment your unique sense of style.


4. Describe any Design Changes You'd Like

Individual design changes can be performed for $10 per change. Changes may include zipper location/number, pocket location/number, type of pockets, wrist adjustment, ribbed cotton locations, collar type, stud locations & any other details you can think of!




5. Tally Your Design Changes and Select Your Leather Type

Cowhide:: Stiff, durable, and takes time to break-in. Provides serious protection and attitude.
Goatskin: Lighter than cow leather and nearly as durable. Worn famously by bomber pilots in WWII for warmth and protection.
Lambskin: Incredibly soft and buttery smooth. Typical choice for modern fashion jackets. Drapes well on the body and takes very little time to break-in.
